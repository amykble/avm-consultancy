About

↓

Our company has been trading for over 8 years, specialising in Instrument and Control Design within the Petro-chemical plant design both onshore and offshore. From FEED Engineering design, through to full EPC projects. With more than fifteen years’ experience in Instrumentation and control system gained in Petro-chemical industries. A proven expertise working on a variety of medium to high complexity projects on producing instrument design deliverables, utilising various software and design tools in delivering to clients’ needs, on time, on budget and consistent with high standards of quality working to BS EN ISO 9001.

We can undertake, Front End Engineering Design, Detailed Engineering, team management and proposal work. Instrument Design capabilities include preparing Technical Bid evaluation, Requisition, CCR Layouts, Location Layouts, Installation Details, Hook-Ups, Cable Schedule, Wiring Termination, and Loops.
